Preamble

DeletedBot strives to protect the data of the users.
We respect your right to have privacy online, and for that reason, we keep tracking and non-useful data collection to zero.
We however need to collect some data to provide basic service functionality, so continue reading this page for all the details.

Information we and our partners collect

Information you share with us

When you submit a form in our website, we collect the form data.
It's used for the basic functionality of the service: without storing such data, the service wouldn't be usable.
All the data you share with us is safe. It will be kept in our databases for an undefined period of time.

Implicit information we get from you

When you browse our website, some information is implicitly sent to us by your browser, such as your IP address.
However, we don't care about that. So we don't save it anywhere in our system.

Our partners

DeletedBot relies on external services to provide its service. You can consult their privacy policies to learn more about what partners do with your data.

What we do with that information

We don't sell, share, distribute, or by any mean, disclose your data.
The only exception is when law enforcement asks us to provide it: in such case, we're obliged and pleased to collaborate with law enforcement.
Your data might be subject to human moderation: in some instances, a trusted service administrator might look into it to detect any potential violation of the Terms.

What to do to get rid of it

We comply with GDPR, CCPA and forks of it. So if you want your data to be removed, write a mail to legal@massivebox.eu.org.
Your data will be removed ASAP.

Policy changes

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any given time, without any prior notice. The last time this policy was updated is: 16 January 2021.
